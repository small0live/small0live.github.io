<p align="justify">
To date, I have conducted research in the areas of <strong>instructional systems</strong> and <strong>artificial social intelligence</strong> to support teamwork, <strong>social behavior</strong> in online platforms, <strong>information visualization</strong> to support decision making, <strong>cognition</strong> and <strong>collaboration</strong> in long duration space missions, the science of <strong>team science</strong>, and <strong>diversity</strong> and <strong>knowledge loss</strong> in open source software development. My research has been accepted in journals, conferences, and workshops associated with leading organizations including <a href="https://www.ieee.org/">IEEE</a>, <a href="https://www.acm.org/">ACM</a>, <a href="https://www.informs.org/">INFORMS</a>, and <a href="https://www.hfes.org/">HFES</a>.</p> 
<p align="justify">
I was a McKnight Doctoral Fellow at the University of Central Florida where I earned a Ph.D. conferred by the College of Engineering and Computer Science. I previously earned a M.Sc. in Modeling and Simulation, a graduate certificate in Cognitive Science, and a B.Sc. in Psychology. 
</p>
<p align="justify">

Fiore</a>, <a href="https://www.eecs.ucf.edu/~gitars/">Gita Sukthankar</a>, <a href="https://kblincoe.github.io/">Kelly Blincoe</a>, <a href="https://www.cs.ucf.edu/~garibay/">Ivan Garibay</a>, and <a href="https://business.ucf.edu/person/carol-saunders/">Carol Saunders</a>. I received the 2022-23 University Award for <a href="https://www.ist.ucf.edu/dr-olivia-newton-ph-d-university-of-central-florida-2022-2023-outstanding-dissertation/"><i>Outstanding Dissertation</i></a> from UCF which recognizes the quality, content, and exceptional contribution of my research.</p>
