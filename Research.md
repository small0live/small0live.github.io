<p align="justify">
Broadly, I study collaborative work and knowledgement management in sociotechnical systems. I have conducted research in the areas of:</p>

<ul>
<li>  <strong>knowledge development</strong> in science teams</li>
<li>  <strong>diversity</strong>, <strong>learning</strong>, and <strong>knowledge loss</strong> in software projects</li>
<li>  <strong>social behavior</strong> and <strong>information spread</strong> in online platforms</li>
<li>  <strong>information visualization</strong> to support decision making</li>
<li>  <strong>team cognition</strong> and <strong>collaboration</strong> in space missions</li>
<li> <strong>artificial social intelligence</strong> for human-machine teams</li>
<li> <strong>instructional systems</strong> to support teamwork</li>
</ul>
<p align = "justify">
As evidence of my interdisciplinary credentials, my research has been accepted in journals and conferences associated with a variety of leading organizations including <a href="https://www.ieee.org/">IEEE</a>, <a href="https://www.acm.org/">ACM</a>, <a href="https://www.informs.org/">INFORMS</a>, and <a href="https://www.hfes.org/">HFES</a>.</p> 

<p align="justify">
I was a <strong>McKnight Doctoral Fellow</strong> at the University of Central Florida where I earned a Ph.D. conferred by the <i>College of Engineering and Computer Science</i>. My dissertation research examining the relationship beween diversity and corporatization in open source software projects was initiated at the <a href="https://www.santafe.edu/">Santa Fe Institute</a> as part of their<a href="https://www.santafe.edu/engage/learn/programs/graduate-workshop-computational-social-science"> Workshop in Computational Social Science</a> and refined with guidance from an interdisciplinary group of mentors: <a href="https://csl.ist.ucf.edu/People">Stephen M. Fiore</a>, <a href="https://www.eecs.ucf.edu/~gitars/">Gita Sukthankar</a>, <a href="https://kblincoe.github.io/">Kelly Blincoe</a>, <a href="https://www.cs.ucf.edu/~garibay/">Ivan Garibay</a>, and <a href="https://business.ucf.edu/person/carol-saunders/">Carol Saunders</a>. </p>
<p align="justify">I received the 2022-23 University Award for <a href="https://www.ist.ucf.edu/dr-olivia-newton-ph-d-university-of-central-florida-2022-2023-outstanding-dissertation/"><i>Outstanding Dissertation</i></a> from UCF which recognizes the quality, content, and exceptional contribution of my research.</p>
